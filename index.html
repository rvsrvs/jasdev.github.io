About

I’m an engineer and writer in New York who is interested in cycling and creating products that help us build better versions of ourselves.

Currently, I spend my days on Peloton’s engineering team. Previously, I built APIs and iOS apps at Tumblr, Imgur, and the Recurse Center (F1 ’15). Before that, I interned at Twitter (KPCB ’13), Fitocracy, and Microsoft while studying mathematics at UVA (SEAS ’14).

If you want to go below the surface, click here.

Posts (RSS, email)

Writing is a way for me to distill thoughts. The intent of these essays and reflective pieces isn’t to teach (I’ve learned that advice is extremely lossy), but rather to reflect and think aloud. While most of these pieces have been drafted alone, it takes a village to publish.
